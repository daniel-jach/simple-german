In the Land of the Giant Carrots Gather as many carrots as possible, because they are worth points. When you have picked up a carrot successfully, it bursts. When Harry's tank is empty, the game is over, so the racing hare should fill up his tank at the fuel pumps. For every second that you ride, you get more points. The opponents come towards Harry and sometimes like riding in groups. Get out of their way, or you could have an accident. The oil spills and the road works are obstacles. If the racing hare rides over an oil spill, you can't steer for about a second. You should also ride around the road works, or the racing hare will fall into them. Duck hunting Try to sit on as many ducks and cakes as possible. Every duck gives you points for the high-score list. Cakes give you energy and extend the game. If Bruno crashes into a robot, he sees stars and the steering is swapped around for five seconds. That means that if you steer left, Bruno runs to the right, and if you steer right, he runs to the left. You should avoid the cactuses. If Bruno sits on one of them, the pain stops him moving for two seconds, wasting valuable time. In the desert A valiant jump into the cool mud gives you points for the high-score list. Earnest has to pick up the water bottles. When the water reserves run out, the game is over. If Earnest runs into a hatey-hare, the steering is swapped around for five seconds That means that if you steer left, Earnest runs to the right, and vice versa. The sand heaps look harmless, but if Earnest runs over one, he falls down and needs a short break. Have fun!
